This lesson will be on tables. Tables have multiple purposes, such as gathering and storing information, or making cards for games like Shadowdale. All of the information is arranged in a "table" tag. Each row in the table is categorized as a "tr" tag. Elements of a table can be then classified into "tr" and "td" tags, and there are many ways of styling tables using CSS. Here is an example of a basic table:
