In this lesson, we will learn how to create a form. First, after making a "form" tag, you make the "fieldset" tag, to incorporate the form. Then, you put a "legend" tag, which will have all off the form info inside of it. Then, you put an "input" tag and give it a type(e.g name, submit, etc.).
